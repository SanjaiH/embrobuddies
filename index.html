Our Range of Products

S.H. (Single Head)

The foundational embroidery machine for focused, high-precision stitching. Ideal for small businesses requiring robust, single-head capability.

S.H. (Variant)

An advanced, specialized version of the Single Head embroidery machine, offering increased stitching speed and custom design integration.

M.H. (Multi Head)

Engineered for complex, multi-layered embroidery designs. Delivers parallel stitching power for high-demand projects and bulk orders.

Laser Cut

Precision laser cutting module designed for flawless, intricate fabric cutting and ultra-fine embroidery preparation.
